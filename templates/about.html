About This App

This AI Story Generator lets you log in, generate creative stories, and save your favorites in a personal library.

How It Works

  • Frontend: HTML, JavaScript, Bootstrap
  • Backend: Flask (Python)
  • AI: Google Gemini
  • Auth: PBKDF2 secure login with session cookies

Features

  • Personal story history per user
  • Favorite / bookmark stories
  • Profile page with stats
