Top 5 Tourist Places in Karnataka

Mysore Palace

A grand royal residence located in Mysuru, famous for its Indo-Saracenic architecture and stunning illumination during festivals.

Known as the City of Palaces

Hampi

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Hampi is filled with ancient temples, ruins, and monuments from the Vijayanagara Empire.

Famous for Virupaksha Temple & Stone Chariot

Coorg (Kodagu)

Known as the "Scotland of India", Coorg is a hill station surrounded by coffee plantations, waterfalls, and lush greenery.

Popular for trekking & coffee estates

Gokarna

A serene coastal town known for its beautiful beaches and ancient temples, offering both spirituality and relaxation.

Om Beach is a major attraction

Jog Falls

One of the highest waterfalls in India, Jog Falls offers breathtaking views, especially during the monsoon season.

Height: ~830 feet
